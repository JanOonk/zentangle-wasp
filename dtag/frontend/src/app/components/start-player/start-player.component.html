How to Start

To play, first create an identity, second validate you are a human, and third request funds for your wallet. Your bet can only be increased during the game. The adjudicator of each round is an IOTA based Smart Contract. These are dev-net tokens and hold no value.

How to Play

The rounds are created and funded by a researcher. Pay close attention to the round objective, and check the guide provided by the researcher. Your bet is automatically placed by the Smart Contract depending on your best chance to win against other players.
